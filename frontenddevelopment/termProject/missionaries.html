Missionary Couples

Couples

We love having missionary couples come to stay with us. Whether you are just staying for a few days or renting a suite, we love to see you! We have many options for our missionary couples.

When you rent an apartment or suite with us, you will get more benefits. Breakfast and dinner will be included in the rent price. You will have access to another room in the hotel, for family to visit. You will also have access to some of our activities. Contact the Front Desk for more information.

Home Away From Home

We want to make your stay here as comfortable as possible. No matter how long it may be. There are suites and apartments availiable to rent for long periods of time.

In the suites, there is one bedroom, a living room, a kitchenette and one bathroom. In the apartments, there are two bedrooms, a living room, a kitchen, and a bathroom. Each room will be cleaned daily unless specified by you.

If you would like to know more, please use the contact us form above.
