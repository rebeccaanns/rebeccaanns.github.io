Our Story

Establised in 2003, Temple Inn & Suites provides its guests with a heavenly experience. We wanted to create a space where people could get away from the world. In our hotel, you are able to be in a comforting home-style room, while also getting to enter the Lord's house.

We created these hotels, in the hopes that others would enjoy being close to the temple, as we do. It can be so hard to find a hotel near a temple. If you do not have a car, it can be near impossible to make it to the temple. We wanted to make both of these things easier for our guests, because we have experienced the stress and disappointment that comes from missing out. We have provided a shuttle to the temple at no extra cost. We also have members of our staff trained to help you get family name cards to take to the temple. These were all things that very extremely important to us when creating our hotels.

We hope you decide to stay with us. We want you to be able to experience all that we have to offer. If there is something you think would improve your stay, let us know! We always want to make our "home" better!
