Reception Hall

Wedding Reception Accomodations

Our reception hall makes it easy to get married in the temple and be close to your venue. You and your guests can stay in the hotel, so you can be close to the temple. The shuttle will be avaliable for use for anyone who would like.

Food Service

One of the biggest decisions you will make for your reception is what food to serve. We hope we can take away some of the stress of this, by providing food with the cost of the venue. We offer a classic, sit-down dinner, a buffet, serve yourself food stations, or a cocktail style reception. You can choose up to two with variations of your choice.

You and your spouse to be will meet with our chef to decide your menu and specific items. You can choose flavors and styles. We will also work around any allergies or dislikes. We hope to make the food something you always remember.

Set Up

We have decorations, tables, guest books, floral arrangements, table numbers, and so much more! Come visit with our designers to see what will best fit your style.

We want you to be as involved as you want when it comes to setting up your reception. If you have specific ideas that you would like done, let us know! If you would like to look at pictures of what we have done in the past and choose from those (or a mix of some), we can do that as well. It is all up to you! Our people will have it all seet up for you as soon as you are ready.
