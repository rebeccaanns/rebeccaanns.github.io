Target Audience

Description

My target audience is people who want to go to Bear Lake and the surrounding areas. It will be directed for families and young adults. It will be any gender. It will mostly target people in the Idaho-Utah area. It will target people of average paygrade. It will give the weather and show activities to do in the town that is listed. It will also show activities to do in areas nearby.

Personas

Persona 1: Sally

Sally is a 23 year old woman. She is single and does not have any kids. She lives in Preston, Idaho. She is on her summer break from college. She loves outdoor activities. She is studying to be a veterinarian. She works at a local grocery store. She has a car.

Persona 2: Sam

Sam is a 35 year old man. He is married and has four young kids. He lives with his family in Soda Springs, Idaho. He works for a company as an IT specialist. He makes an average yearly income. His family loves to play together and go on vacations. They have a family car.

Scenarios

Sally uses her phone to look up the weather while at work. She wants to go to the beach when she gets off. She needs to find the hourly weather.

Sam and his family are looking at the weather. They see that it is too cold to go to the beach, but they still want to do something as a family. They would like to see some ideas of activities in the area.

Sally has friends coming into town to go on a vacation. They want weather specifically for the area they are visiting. They want something that will quickly let them know when they can go to the beach.

Some of Sam's kids do not know how to read yet. They want to just look at the pictures to see what the weather will be like this week.

Sally just wants a quick view of what thee temperature is at that current moment.
